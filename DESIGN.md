# DESIGN.md — Easy E’s Detailing fresh redesign

## Product personality
Youth-owned neighborhood detailing with surprising polish: affordable, hungry, sporty, direct, and local. It should feel like a redline garage poster crossed with a tap-to-book mobile app.

## Audience / use context
Parents, neighbors, students, truck owners, and everyday drivers around Troutman who see the business card or link on a phone and need a fast answer: what does he clean, what does it cost, how do I text him?

## Inspiration references
- **Wings Mobile Detailing:** booking-first mobile service, “we come to you,” service confidence.
- **Lakeside Mobile Detail:** Lake Norman-area local trust, process steps, stat cards.
- **E1 Auto Spa / red-black auto style:** dark garage atmosphere, red performance lighting, glossy visual energy.
- **Uploaded business card:** black/red/white/chrome palette, SUV/interior imagery, hex icons, red dividers, pricing table, youth-local banner.

## Visual direction
- **Mood:** night garage, red LED strips, chrome shine, clean water/soap highlights, not generic SaaS.
- **Layout:** mobile-first booking page with a fixed action bar, an oversized “detail ticket” hero, route-line sections, and diagonal red dividers.
- **Color:** black charcoal base, fire red, chrome white/silver, tiny blue-white soap sparkle accents.
- **Typography:** heavy condensed display for logo/price moments; clean sans for readable booking info.
- **Components:** sticky call/text bar, service “wash passes,” pricing pit-board, local proof strip, business-card proof image, process route.
- **Motion/interactions:** red scanner line, shimmering chrome title, floating soap sparks, reveal-on-scroll, interactive card tilt.

## Anti-patterns to avoid
- No generic centered SaaS hero.
- No vague “premium detail” copy that hides the actual prices.
- No overcomplicated form; Easton’s conversion path is call/text.
- Do not make it look like an expensive ceramic-coating shop. Keep it affordable and youth-local.

## Mobile behavior
Mobile is the primary composition: action bar always reachable, phone number visible, pricing cards big enough to screenshot, business card image included as credibility/source tie-in.
